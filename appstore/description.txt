Watch out! Tiny archers are shooting your wrist, but luckily the arrows always point to the current time! Can you help them hit the centre?

A fun animated watchface, compass and game with secrets to discover.

Features:
    Analogue time; long minute arrow, short hour arrow
    Low battery (<20%) warning; the minute arrow always hits the outermost ring.
    Bluetooth disconnect warning; vibe and the hour arrow pierces through the target, leaving behind a large black hole
    Compass; shake to shoot a red arrow which arrives from the north

Instructions:
 - Shake to enable the red compass arrow
 - Quickly turn your Pebble to align the hour and minute arrows with North as they arrive to score a perfect hit.
 - Get both arrows perfectly in the centre together for a small surprise.
 - There's also a random chance to hit the centre regardless; some say luck is also a skill...

Supports all pebbles, but it's perfect for the new big-colour-screen Time 2 and especially Round 2!

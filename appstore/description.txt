Watch out! Tiny archers are shooting your wrist, but luckily the arrows always point to the current time! Can you help them hit the centre?

A fun animated watchface, with a small game and other secrets to discover.

Instructions:
 - Shake to reshoot new arrows, then align each one with compass North just before they arrive to hit the centre.
 - Get two perfect hits for a small surprise.
 - There's also a random chance to hit the centre regardless; some say luck is also a skill...

Supports all pebbles*, but it's perfect for the new big-colour-screen Time 2 and especially Round 2!
Note the compass is turned off to save power unless you shake, so this still works well as just a watchface.

Planned updates:
 - More ways to win
 - More fun special effects
 - Customisation options (more/less vibes, enable second hand, change target face / arrow colours, etc.)
 - Boring stuff (battery indicator etc.)

*the original Pebble 2 does not have a compass, so that aspect won't work.

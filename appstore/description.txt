Watch out! Tiny archers are shooting your wrist, but luckily the arrows always point to the current time!
Their aim is a bit off, but if you can figure out how to help them you may be handsomely rewarded.

A crisp, fully animated and fun watchface with secrets. Supports all pebbles, but it's perfect for the new big-colour-screen Time 2 and especially Round 2!

Instructions:
 - The long arrow is minutes; the short, hours.
 - Shake/tap to reshoot.
 - Hit the centre by fulfilling secret conditions; think about the hardware features your pebble has, and experiment to find them!
 - Getting closer to the condition makes the arrows more accurate, but there is a small threshold to get a perfect hit every time.
 - There's also a random chance to hit the centre regardless; some say luck is also a skill...

Aside from random chance, there is currently 1 perfect hit condition, for which I've left a clue in this appstore listing! Please check back in a few days; I will quickly add 2 more and a final secret unlock for anyone who wins all 4 trophies. (edit: another clue - the first trophy is not possible to achieve on Pebble 2, but it works on Pebble 2 Duo, Time 2 and all other platforms.)

I'll also be making this app open-source, leaving the (maybe obfuscated) code as the final clue for those brave enough ;)
